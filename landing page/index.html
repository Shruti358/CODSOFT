Color & Typography

Focus on Clean, Non‑Overlapping Design

A good landing page isn’t just about fancy effects. It’s mostly about clarity: clear sections, consistent spacing, and elements that never overlap or fight for attention.

Use simple CSS rules like display: flex, max-width, and box-sizing: border-box to keep things under control while you experiment with creative layouts.

Checklist

  • No text touching screen edges
  • Consistent section padding (top & bottom)
  • Buttons easy to see and click
  • Readable fonts on all backgrounds
